Who We Are

A dedicated education consultancy, not a side service

Range Global Education (Pvt) Ltd works in collaboration and partnership with international student recruitment agents and universities around the world, offering programmes and services in international education to Sri Lankan students and beyond — through professional counselling.

We know that behind every application is a family making one of the biggest decisions of their lives. That's why our process is built around clarity, honesty and support at every stage — not just placement.
